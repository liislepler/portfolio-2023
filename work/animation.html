The History of Coffee

As part of the course Motion Graphics, I had a group project to make a short animation video. We decided to make a fun video about the history of coffee highlighting the funny stories surrounding the beans journey becoming one of the most loved drinks. We used After Effects and Photoshop.

Co-creators: Nicki Mirani, Elin Tri, Thishaya Ukwatta

THE WORLD OF PLANTS

As part of the course Client-side Programming, I had a group project to make a game using JavaScript and JavaScript libraries. We chose to make a digital board game where our inspiraions were Snakes & Ladders, The Circus Game, and Animal Crossing. Before this project, we had studied the basics of JavaScript and done small

games but for this project we really had expand our programming skills. While making this game, I had first-time experience with Three.js and making 3D animated objects. The game was met with a lot of good feedback and received the highest grade.

Co-creator: Elin Tri
