Who am I?

My name is Liis, an Estonian living in Sweden. Art has always been a way to express myself. I spent my childhood drawing imaginary scenarios and later on studied art full-time in high school. Even though most of my life I have been surrounded by traditional art, I became more interested in how I could put my artistic vision into design. And I went to study New Media Design at Jönköping University. This portfolio is a collection of my work done over the years of my studies. Enjoy the ride…
